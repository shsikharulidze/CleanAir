Breathe better indoors

Secondhand smoke is not just your choice. It harms the people around you and it harms you more than you think. Even short exposure raises risk for heart disease and stroke.

Source: CDC

Get help to quit Know the rules
Important: No smoking or vaping in indoor public spaces. Keep distance from building entrances to protect everyone's health.

Quitting helps immediately

Smoking damages nearly every organ. Quitting lowers risk quickly and keeps falling over time. Free support and medication can double your success rates.

Source: CDC
