National Quitline

1-800-QUIT-NOW

Free coaching in many languages, 24/7 support, and personalized quit plan

  • Available 24 hours a day
  • Support in multiple languages
  • Free telephone counseling
  • Online resources and tools

NY State Quitline

1-866-NY-QUITS

Free support and often free patches, gum, or lozenges for New York residents

  • Free nicotine replacement therapy
  • Personalized coaching
  • Text message support
  • Online quit plan tools

Quit Tools & Medication

Nicotine Replacement

  • Nicotine patches
  • Gum and lozenges
  • Nasal spray
  • Inhalers

Many available over-the-counter or free through quitlines

Prescription Medications

  • Varenicline (Chantix)
  • Bupropion (Zyban)
  • Other FDA-approved options

Talk to your healthcare provider about prescription options

Apps & Online Tools

  • SmokefreeTXT text messages
  • quitSTART app
  • Online quit guides
  • Progress tracking tools

Many free digital tools available to support your quit journey

Tips for Success

Before You Quit

  • Tell family and friends your quit date
  • Remove all tobacco products from your home and car
  • Stock up on healthy snacks and water
  • Plan activities for your first smoke-free day

During Cravings

  • Remember cravings only last 3-5 minutes
  • Take deep breaths
  • Drink water or chew gum
  • Call your quitline for support

Stay Motivated

  • Focus on the health benefits
  • Calculate money saved
  • Reward yourself for milestones
  • Remember you're protecting your family
