Common misconceptions about smoking, vaping, and secondhand smoke exposure

Myth

I only smoke near windows so the air is fine

Fact

Smoke drifts and lingers indoors. Fine particles travel and stick to surfaces. People nearby still breathe it.

Fact

Remember: There is no safe level of exposure

Even brief exposure to secondhand smoke can cause immediate harm to the heart and blood vessels. The only way to protect yourself and others is to maintain completely smoke-free indoor environments.

Source: CDC
